LADAK

Ladakh is most famous for breathtaking landscapes, the crystal clear skies, the highest mountain passes, thrilling adventure activities, Buddhist Monasteries and festivals. Not only is it the only place in India where the graceful Double Hump camels found, but it is also the place where you will find the Magnetic Hill, the one-of-a-kind phenomena that defies gravity. Trekking on the high mountains is one of the most sought after adventures of people visiting Ladakh on routes like Chadar Trek, Stock Kangri Trek, Markha Valley Trek, Nubra Valley Trek, Padum to Darcha route, etc. The dangerous heights and the risky valleys by the route make it one of the most famous trek routes to accomplish for adventurers.
  2. Ladakh finds a lot of fame for permitting people to trek on a frozen river which is called the Chadar Trek route, a rare adventure to partake in.
  3. The residents of Ladakh make it famous for being the most hospitable and warm to entertain every tourist who visits their land.
  4. Camping by the lakes and valleys like Pangong, Tso Moriri and Nubra Valley adds to the list of reasons why Ladakh is famous.
  5. Ladakh is also famous for offering the best of meditative rejuvenation in its monasteries enclosed by beautifully carved walls. It is therefore believed that one feels at their spiritual best when in Ladakh.

Explore Paradise Island


Welcome to Paradise Island, a stunning tropical destination that offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and vibrant culture. With its pristine beaches, lush landscapes, and rich history, this destination is a dream getaway for every traveler.

  1. Sunset Cove: Experience breathtaking sunsets at this picturesque cove.
  2. Historical Ruins: Explore ancient ruins that tell the story of the island's fascinating past.
  3. Adventure Trails: Hike through lush trails and discover hidden waterfalls.
  4. Tropical Gardens: Stroll through vibrant gardens with exotic flora and fauna.
  5. Beachfront Resorts: Relax in luxury at world-class beachfront resorts.
  • Island Seafood Platter: Indulge in a variety of fresh seafood caught daily.
  • Tropical Fruit Delight: Savor the sweetness of local fruits like mangoes and pineapples.
  • Spicy Coconut Curry: Experience the flavors of authentic island cuisine with a hint of spice.
  • Beachside Barbecue: Enjoy a barbecue feast with ocean views and sea breeze.
